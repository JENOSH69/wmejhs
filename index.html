About Us

Journey

Established on 10th February 1994, Western Modern English Junior High School is a private, co-educational institution nestled in the serene village of Darap, West Sikkim. Surrounded by spiritual landmarks—Pemayangtse Monastery, Nedham Cave, Ranidhunga, and Khechuperi Lake— our school lies in the peaceful lap of Mount Kanchenjunga, offering an ideal environment for learning and personal growth. Founded by Mr. and Mrs. Tarun Bahadur Ghishing, along with dedicated community leaders, the school began with just 25 students in a rented house. Through unwavering duty, devotion, and determination, it has grown into a thriving institution known for providing quality, value-based education. Since relocating to its current site, known as Lung-jong Kemba (High Rock), in 2001, the school has transformed from a humble paddy field into a center of learning for hundreds of students from across the state. We follow a strong foundational curriculum based on the ICSE pattern, introducing children to learning from the age of three to prepare them for future academic success. Our vision is to nurture responsible citizens with knowledge, morals, and leadership qualities. With a future plan of expanding into a full-fledged CBSE-affiliated secondary school, we remain committed to shaping young minds for a brighter tomorrow.
